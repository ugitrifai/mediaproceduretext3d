Materi Pokok: Procedure Text

Definisi: Teks yang menjelaskan atau memandu pembaca tentang cara membuat, mengoperasikan, atau melakukan sesuatu melalui serangkaian langkah terstruktur.

Struktur Umum (Generic Structure):

  • Goal/Aim: Maksud/tujuan utama (biasanya tertera pada judul teks).
  • Materials/Ingredients: Alat, bahan, atau komponen yang diperlukan.
  • Steps/Methods: Urutan kronologis langkah-langkah tindakan nyata.

Unsur Kebahasaan:

  • Menggunakan Simple Present Tense.
  • Menggunakan Imperative Sentences (Kalimat perintah, contoh: Connect, Push, Turn on).
  • Menggunakan Temporal Connectives (Kata hubung waktu, contoh: First, Next, Then, Finally).

Contoh Teks Prosedur (Manual)

Title / Goal: How to Connect a Laptop to an External Projector

Materials: A laptop, an LCD projector, and a VGA or HDMI cable.

Steps:

  1. First, plug the power cable of the projector into a working wall outlet.
  2. Second, connect the HDMI or VGA cable to the video port of your laptop.
  3. Then, plug the other end of the cable into the corresponding input port on the projector.
  4. Next, turn on the projector by pressing the power button and wait for it to warm up.
  5. After that, turn on your laptop and press the projection shortcut key (usually Fn + F4 or Win + P).
  6. Finally, choose the "Duplicate" or "Extend" mode to project your screen properly.
